About This Space

Omnia cum anima
sapientia sunt.

Welcome to Animasapient, a synthesis of: anima (soul) and sapient (wisdom), as this is a website to keep a wise soul.

Here you will find explorations of philosophy, ancient and contemporary, explorations of religion and spirituality across traditions, studies of the world's geography, and the ways landscape shapes civilization, and reflections on culture in all its extraordinary diversity.

This is a personal project, and thus may seem small scale.

Enjoy Animasapient and a path to wisdom in what makes the World tick.
